Restaurant Reviews

Seoul Korean BBQ and Sushi

Seoul Korean BBQ and Sushi is a Korean BBQ Restaurant located in New Britain, CT and is one of only a handfull of Korean Restaurants in the state of CT. The specialize in authentic korean cuisine and as part of the experience, includes grilling tables for guest to grill their own foods to their liking. They also specialize in many different types of sushi, including specialty rolls created daily by trained chefs.

Umami Japanese Restaurant

Umami Japanese Restaurant is a traditional japanese restaurant located in Vernon, CT. It is a local favorite for many different types of sushi, however, they do specialize in traditional japanese cuisine.

The Cheesecake Factory

The Cheesecake Factory is a ver popular restaurant that has many locations nationwide, but the one that I frequent is located in West Hartford, CT. Though they are known for their world famous cheesecakes, they are also known for their amazing italian cuisine. One of my true favorites from the menu is the Chicken Marsala.
